About Me

Hello, my major is in electronics, yet I unexpectedly have a website. The reason behind this is the evolving artificial intelligence and the ominous claims that social media platforms are undergoing a radical transformation. In short, it's an absurd assumption that in the future, social media platforms will collapse, and everyone will possess a level of knowledge equivalent to at least HTML, as if it were a basic skill in computing. I cannot predict what the future holds, but I am certain that one day, looking back, opening this website will remind me of how much fun I had, and that thought will bring me joy.
